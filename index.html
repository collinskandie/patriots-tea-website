About Us

Our Story

Leading its incredible team is a local woman, a celebrated tea professional with over two decades of industry experience and distinguished trailblazer in the tea industry, who has dedicated her entire career to mastering the art and science of tea, from manufacture to market. Her remarkable journey - commenced as Management Trainee overseeing tea manufacture at the Tea Factories then active Tea Trading at the Mombasa Tea Auction to leading Kenya's Specialty Tea Sub-Sector.

Patriots Teas competitiveness is drawn from its team of experts endowed with wealth of knowledge & competence in innovative tea products development, blending & pairing, organoleptic sensory evaluation (tea tasting), tea target marketing, tea sale contract negotiation, overall tea education and stakeholder engagement.

Inspired by its large, envisioned future, the Company is devoted to empowering tea farmers, impacting women and showcasing Kenya’s finest teas to the world.

  • Our Mission : To celebrate Kenya’s diverse tea heritage while fostering sustainable livelihoods through fair trade, innovation, and agro-tourism.
  • Our Dual Mission Tea Excellence: To redefine tea through innovation, quality, and heritage, celebrating Kenya’s renowned heritage & specialty teas on a local and global scale. Agro-Tourism Leadership To transform Kericho into a premier destination for tea enthusiasts, offering immersive experiences that blend culture and the art of tea.

What We Offer

Tea

PATRIOTS Teas of Origin specializes in Black CTC, Orthodox, and Specialty Teas, including:

  • Purple Tea: Rich in anthocyanins, promoting wellness.
  • Green Tea: Refreshing blends with health-enhancing properties.
  • Yellow Tea, White Tea, Silver Needles, and exclusive Oolong Tea blends

All crafted to perfection for tea connoisseurs.

Each cup tells a story, blending tradition with modern flavors, from fruit and herb-infused green teas to botanical-rich purple teas.

Agro-Tourism

PATRIOTS Teas of Origin is redefining Kericho as a must-visit agro-tourism destination. Visitors can immerse themselves in the enchanting teascapes through:

  • Guided Tea Estate Tours: Walk through the rolling tea gardens, learn about cultivation, and meet the farmers behind the magic.
  • Tea Tasting & Drinking Sessions: Experience the art of tea tasting with expertly curated flights of specialty teas.
  • Cultural Experiences: Enjoy local cuisines, folk music, and dance that celebrate Kenya’s rich heritage.
  • Educational Workshops: Learn about sustainable farming practices, tea crafting, and the science behind every brew.
